# DeadWoods

**Tags:** The-Island, Port-of-Night-City, Boostergang, Smuggling, DeadWoods, Crew-Neutral, Organized-Crime

**Type:** Gang
**Territory:** Port of Night City (The Island)
**Threat Level:** Medium-High

## Overview

DeadWoods is a gang operating in the Port of Night City area during the Time of the Red. They are one of the notable criminal organizations that have carved out territory in this waterfront combat zone.

## Description

The gang has established themselves among the warehouses and docks of the Port area, making their living through smuggling, cargo theft, and various illegal waterfront operations. They compete with other gangs for control of the valuable shipping infrastructure.

The Port of Night City is a dangerous area, full of warehouses, flophouses, smash-houses, and docks. Legitimate businesses survive only by catering to gang members and criminals. DeadWoods is one of the groups that maintains a presence in this lawless region.

## Territory

- Port of Night City
- Dock 13 area
- Surrounding warehouses

## Allies/Enemies

- **Enemies:** Consortium (rival gang in Port of Night City)
- **Competing with:** Various other Port-based groups
