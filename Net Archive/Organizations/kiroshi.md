# Kiroshi Opticals

**Tags:** Corporation, Cyberware, Japanese, Tech

**Type:** Corporate (Japanese Megacorporation)  
**Era:** Time of the Red (2040s)

## Overview

Kiroshi Opticals (also known as Matsushima-Kiroshi Optics or simply Kiroshi) is a Japanese corporation specializing in optical cyberware and cybernetics. Founded in Kamisuwa, Nagano Prefecture, Japan, Kiroshi is widely regarded as the leading expert in producing optical cyberware and maintains a significant presence in Night City.

## History

### Origins

The corporation was originally founded as a watch manufacturer in Kamisuwa, Nagano, expanding in the 1980s. In 2013, the group changed its name from "Kiro-shiya" to "Kiroshi." It received its biggest boost by launching an orbital factory with the aid of the Japan Aerospace Bureau (JAB) and Kenjiri Technology.

### Pre-War Era

By 2020, Kiroshi was aiming to dominate the Asian market. The corporation has major offices in Tokyo, Osaka, Seattle, Night City, Washington D.C., Hong Kong, Shanghai, and Singapore. Kiroshi products can be found worldwide, from smaller shops to large electronic chains.

During the Fourth Corporate War, Kiroshi had a brief conflict with the European Zeiss company. After signing a peace treaty, the two rivals divided their spheres of influence but remained in a state of cold war, spying on one another.

### Time of the Red (2040s)

During the RED era, Kiroshi maintained its Night City presence despite the corporate consolidation following the Fourth Corporate War. The corporation continued to produce its iconic line of cyberoptic implants, including:

- **Kiroshi Eyecam X** — Standard issue cyberoptic camera
- **Kiroshi MonoVision** — Budget-friendly single-eye cyberoptic
- **Kiroshi OptiShield** — Protective optical cyberware

Kiroshi is known for shrewd advertising, sponsoring celebrities and pop artists like Us Cracks, known for their unique eye implants.

## Night City Operations

### Facilities

Kiroshi maintains a corporate office in Night City, serving as a major hub for North American operations. During the Time of the Red, their products are available through authorized ripperdocs throughout the city.

### Products in RED Era

- Optical cyberware (cyberoptics)
- Kiroshi Eyecam series
- MonoVision single-eye implants
- OptiShield protective optics
- Optical HUD systems

## Reputation

Kiroshi is considered a reliable mid-tier corporation in the cyberware market. Their products are known for:
- Quality optical systems
- Affordable options for lower-income clients
- Wide distribution network
- Popularity among fashion-conscious edgerunners
