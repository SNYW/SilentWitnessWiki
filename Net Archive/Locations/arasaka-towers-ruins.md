# Arasaka Towers Ruins

**Tags:** Hot-Zone, The-Island, Combat-Zone, Threat-High, Arasaka, Historical-Site, Radiation-Hazard, Johnny-Silverhand, The-Time-of-the-Red

**District:** Hot Zone - Old Corporate Center (The Island)
**Threat Rating:** Combat (Highest)

## Overview

The Arasaka Towers Ruins are all that remains of the once-mighty Arasaka Corporation headquarters in Night City. The twin towers were the target of Johnny Silverhand's legendary attack in 2023, which culminated in a nuclear explosion that devastated the entire central business district.

## Description

What stands today is a twisted memorial to corporate ambition gone wrong. The nuclear blast sheared off the upper floors of both towers, leaving jagged stumps of steel and concrete. The lobby, once a monument to Arasaka's power, is now a tomb of shattered marble and rusted metal.

The towers remain highly radioactive, though levels have decreased since the immediate aftermath of the blast. Arasaka still maintains a keen interest in the site, though they have no official presence in the ruins.

## Historical Significance

- **2023 Attack:** Site of Johnny Silverhand's nuclear attack during the Fourth Corporate War
- **Corporate War Catalyst:** The destruction of Arasaka Towers marked a turning point in the Fourth Corporate War
- **Symbol of the Red:** The ruins serve as a constant reminder of the devastation of the Time of the Red

## Notable Features

- **Radiation Hazard:** Still highly radioactive, though levels have diminished over 20+ years
- **Corporate Secrets:** Rumors persist of hidden vaults and data caches buried in the rubble
- **Pilgrimage Site:** Some fans of Johnny Silverhand make pilgrimages to the site

## Organizations

- Arasaka (unofficial monitoring)
- Various scavver groups
