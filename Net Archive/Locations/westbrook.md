# Westbrook

**Tags:** Westbrook, District, Corporate-Zone, Threat-Low

**District Code:** F  
**Type:** Executive Residential Zone  
**Threat Rating:** Low

## Overview

Westbrook is an exclusive residential district in northern Night City, known for its luxury homes, executive zones, and high-end amenities. Originally developed as an upscale suburb in the late 20th century, Westbrook weathered the Fourth Corporate War better than many other Night City districts.

By the 2040s, Westbrook had become the premier destination for Night City's wealthy elite, corporate executives, and well-to-do families. The district features sprawling estates, luxury megabuildings, private security, and exclusive amenities that cater to those who can afford the premium lifestyle.

## Government

- **City Manager:** Appointed by Night City Council
- **Security Provider:** Private corporate security, MaxTac (rapid response)
- **Gangs Present:** Minimal (high security deters most criminal activity)

## Notable Locations

### Executive Zone
The heart of Westbrook's corporate and residential activity, featuring luxury offices and homes for top executives.

### Luxury Megabuildings
High-end residential towers with premium amenities, including private security, concierge services, and state-of-the-art cyberware maintenance.

### High-End Entertainment
Exclusive clubs, restaurants, and braindance theaters catering to Night City's wealthy residents.

### Golf Courses and Parks
Several exclusive golf courses and well-maintained public parks provide green space in the urban environment.

## Economy

Westbrook's economy is driven by:
- Executive residential housing
- Corporate executive offices
- Luxury retail and services
- Private security contracting
- High-end entertainment

## Notes

Westbrook represents the stark inequality of Night City during the RED era. While most of the city struggles with reconstruction and economic hardship, Westbrook thrives as a haven for the wealthy.
