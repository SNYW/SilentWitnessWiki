# Maelstrom Arena

**Tags:** Maelstrom-Arena, Watson, Northside-Industrial-District, Maelstrom, Fight-Club, Underground-Fight-Club, Threat-High, Combat-Zone

**Type:** Underground Fight Club / Entertainment Venue  
**District:** Northside Industrial District, Watson  
**Threat Rating:** High

## Overview

The Maelstrom Arena is an underground fight club and entertainment venue located in the Northside Industrial District (NID) of Watson. Operated by the Maelstrom gang, the arena hosts illegal boxing, cyberboxing, and "death matches" for profit.

## History

### Origins

The arena was established in 2035 when Maelstrom took control of an abandoned warehouse in the NID. What started as informal fights between gang members evolved into a profitable underground spectacle attracting edgerunners, gamblers, and corporate scouts looking for talent.

### RED Era Operations

By 2045, the Maelstrom Arena has become one of the most famous illegal fight venues in Night City. The fights draw crowds from across the city, with some bouts being broadcast via illegal netfeeds to underground betting rings.

## Fight Types

### Cybersport Boxing
- Modified boxing matches with minimal cyberware restrictions
- Most popular event type
- Heavy betting action

### Death Matches
- No-holds-barred fights to submission or death
- Rare events, only for high-stakes bouts
- Often feature cyberpsychos or volunteers

### Corporate Scout Nights
- Secret events where corporate talent spotters evaluate fighters
- Successful fighters may be recruited by Militech or Arasaka
- These events are invitation-only

## Notable Features

- Reinforced octagonal fighting cage
- Medical station for treating injuries (basic)
- Betting booths with secure transaction kiosks
- VIP viewing rooms for high-rollers
- Netfeed broadcasting equipment

## Rules

- No weapons in the cage
- Cyberware restrictions vary by match type
- Referees are Maelstrom members
- Winners take 60% of purse, 40% goes to house

## Notes

- Extremely dangerous - death is common in death matches
- Not recommended for edgerunners without proper preparation
- Maelstrom controls all aspects of the venue
- Corporate scouts sometimes attend, offering potential employment
- The arena is a major source of income for Maelstrom
