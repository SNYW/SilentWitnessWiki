# Reclamation Zone

**District Code:** K  
**Type:** Rebuilding Urban Zone  
**Threat Rating:** Medium

## Overview

A small enclave run by nomads turned reclaimers. They've taken over the operation of Night City's public transportation, exchanging their technical and motor skills for the land.

## Government

- **City Manager:** Santos Dorado (elected by district residents)
- **Security Provider:** Los Perros Guardianes
- **Gangs Present:** Tombstone Preservers

## Locations

### (K1) Herschel's Crematorium
A funeral home run by an undertaker who truly believes everyone deserves a dignified sendoff into the next life.
