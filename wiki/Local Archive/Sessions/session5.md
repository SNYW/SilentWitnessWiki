# Session 5: Wait for the Drop

**Tags:** Session, Session-5, Upper-Marina, Sparks-Club, Tesla-Tyrants, Infiltration, Vehicle-Chase

## TL;DR

The crew gears up to hit the Tesla Tyrants at their night club in Upper Marina called **Sparks**. After securing wheels from Old Boy's chop shop contacts, they route through Little China but get harassed by NCPD along the way. Roadblock scores intel on the Tesla Tyrants' leader from his day job, while Magpie rallies the crew toward their goal — stealing a high-powered battery for his R.O.O.K. project.

## Wheels of Fortune

The crew hangs tight at **Vultures Nest**, the Tesla spy's corpse still frozen in the cryo unit like a Thanksgiving turkey waiting to be carved. **Magpie** lays it out plain: they're going to hit the Teslas, hit them hard, and walk away with a battery powerful enough to bring R.O.O.K. to life — his miniature tank project built from that sweet, sweet cryo unit.

Problem is, the target's in Upper Marina. Three-hour walk through terrain that wants to kill you, or a forty-minute drive through streets that aren't exactly welcoming.

**Old Boy** makes a call. His old pals **Belinda** and **Jacky** run a chop shop nearby — the kind of place where cars go to die and come back meaner. They got a beat-to-shit dune buggy with their name on it. Only catch? Old Boy's gotta come visit. Man's been a ghost, and they're not letting him forget it.

He goes. Gets the wheels.

Meanwhile, **Roadblock** swings by **Precipice Securities**. Catches up with the boys, then corners his boss, **Danny Roman**, for the real talk. Danny's got the file on the Tesla Tyrants' head honcho: **Volta**. Never leaves the house. Never needs to. She's got eyes everywhere — cameras, enough surveillance gear to make MaxTac jealous. Danny hands it over only after Roadblock promises it'll score them the big one. Roadblock takes the file and leaves.

## Wake Up Call

Back at the Nest, **Sparrowhawk** wakes up screaming. Not a nightmare — his old man. Gone. **Magpie** doesn't do comfort. Man's dead, kid. You're one of us now. Get back to work.

**Bloam** wanders out like she owns the place. Sits next to Sparrowhawk. Just sits there.

**Roadblock** walks in. Sees Bloam near the kid. Doesn't think — just *moves*. Snatches Sparrowhawk away,slams 100eb into the kid's hand, and tells him to stay far away from "that thing." Kid takes the cash. Can't blame him.

**Old Boy** rolls up with the dune buggy. **Magpie** tells Sparrowhawk to stay with **Bekah** and keep an eye on Bloam. The kid agrees — but keeps his distance. Then, because this is Night City and nothing's free, he negotiates a babysitting fee. For Bloam. Magpie tells him no advance payments. They got a job to do.

They roll out.

## Pigs and Fines

The crew picks the route through **Little China** — safer than the Combat Zones, longer way around. Fine. They can work with that.

Halfway to Upper Marina, lights flash behind them. NCPD.

The pigs pile out. Demands ID, questions everyone, Inspects the vehicle like they're looking for weapons of mass destruction. Things get tense when **Roadblock** and **Old Boy** step out — suddenly every cop's got a gun drawn, screaming at them to get on the ground.

Situation cools. Party explains where they're headed and why. Cops make a note: they're traveling with a known gang member. Since nobody's actually broken any laws worth dying for, they settle on a 150eb ticket.

**Old Boy** gets tagged for letting an uninsured driver (Roadblock) behind the wheel.

Pigs.

The crew resolved to pay the fine and keeps rolling toward **Sparks**.
