# Session 6: Sparks and Stripes

**Tags:** Session, Session-6, Upper-Marina, Sparks-Club, Tesla-Tyrants, Infiltration, Combat

## TL;DR

The crew hits **Sparks**, the **Tesla Tyrants**' club in Upper Marina. They sneak in through the back, scope out the layout, and hit the bar. But the bartender's onto them — slips them a Mickey Finn. **Magpie** goes down hard, **Roadblock** gets weak and suggestible. **Old Boy** pops a flashbang to create a distraction, accesses the terminal, kills the cameras and opens a secret door. The team fights back, doses **Roadblock** with RapiDetox, drops the barmaids, and prepares to take on the bartender and his chromed dancer while **Old Boy** goes solo against **Volta** in the depths below.

## The Approach

The crew finally rolls up to **Sparks** in their borrowed dune buggy. Upper Marina ain't pretty, but the club's got that Tesla sheen — all chrome and neon, like a temple to the EV gods.

They cruise around back, find an alley entrance that leads to the back door. Sneaky. The door pops into the can — two stalls, sinks, the works. Plus two electrically locked rooms. **Magpie**, **Roadblock**, and **Sal** get one open — terminal inside, cameras watching everything. The other room? Probably storage or some gonk's office.

**Roadblock**, being **Roadblock**, uses the downtime to take a massive shit. Man's gotta decompress.

**Old Boy** hangs back near the toilets. He's thinking — flashbang the cameras, create a distraction, get to that terminal. But flashbanging the internal cameras? Dumb. Everyone sees it. Instead, he figures: flashbang an external camera, get **Volta**'s attention on the feed, use that chaos to slip in and hit the terminal.

## The Party

The three head into the main floor. **Sparks** is bumping — hardbass thumping, lights strobing, bodies grinding. In the middle of it all, a dancer: **Julie**. She's heavily chromed — arms, torso, the works. But the highlight? Her legs end in points. Like needles. Some kind of cyber-enhancement or just fashion gone wrong. She works the stage like a chrome goddess while the crowd loses their minds.

The three grab a booth, act normal, watch the show. Eventually, they migrate to the bar.

Bartender's a slick dude. Eyes **Roadblock** for a second too long. Fucker thinks he recognizes him from somewhere — old NCPD files maybe? **Roadblock**'s face is hard to forget. The bartender taps his earpiece, has a quiet chat, then slides three drinks down the bar. "First round's on the house. Let's talk parley."

## The Offer

Free drinks? In Night City? Should've known it was too good to be true.

The bartender thinks they're there to make a trade. "You bringing Adam for the exchange, right?"

**Adam.** The Tesla spy from back in the day. The one they iced, froze, and scavved. They got nothing to trade — the guy's a corpse.

**Magpie** plays it cool. "We'll exchange Adam for a battery." Classic **Magpie** — always angling. But he pushes further: wants to talk to **Volta** directly.

Bartender shakes his head. "**Volta** don't do face-to-face. You want in, you go through me."

Team says nope. They go back to watching Julie shake her chrome ass on stage.

That's when the drinks hit.

## The Drop

The poison kicks in fast. **Magpie** slumps — out cold, slithering off the barstool like a puppet with cut strings.

**Roadblock** fights it longer, but he's going down too. He's awake, barely — body heavy, mind foggy, totally suggestible. Bartender's guys are already moving in to drag them both out.

**Sal** sees what's happening. Fast. Pulls out a **RapiDetox** shot, tries to slam it into **Magpie**. But hands are shaking, the hit misses — the kid's still metabolizing the poison, just slower.

Bartender strolls over. "You two, take them to the back. You—" he points at **Sal**. "You're coming too."

## The Break

Right then — chaos.

**Old Boy** pops a flashbang on an external camera. *BANG!* **Volta**'s watching, everyone's watching. The distraction is perfect. **Old Boy** bolts into the terminal room.

Bartender sprints outside to check the damage.

**Sal** moves fast. Gets to **Roadblock**, doses him up — perfect hit this time. **Roadblock**'s eyes snap open. He's back. He's *pissed*.

Inside the terminal room, **Old Boy**'s ripping through the system. Cameras off. Lights out. Hidden door in the back — opens up to some underground tunnel. Unknown territory. He doesn't care. He's going in.

## The Fight

The team recovers fast. **Magpie** is still wobbling but on his feet. **Roadblock** is ready to throw hands. **Sal** pulls out a road flare — lights up the room like a firefight.

Two barmaids are dragging the drugged party toward the back. Team drops them both — clean hits, both unconscious.

**Old Boy** vaults over the bar and dives through the secret door. Alone. Going solo. His mission? Kill or capture **Volta**.

Bartender bursts back inside. Club's in chaos — people running, screaming, trampling each other to get out. He yells for **Julie**. She emerges from wherever she was hiding — behind the stage, under a table, who knows. Girl's got survival instincts.

She steps up. Cyberlegs crackling with electricity. Points glinting under the lights. She takes a swipe at **Roadblock** — fast, precise, deadly.

The party readies up. Bartender on one side, Julie on the other. **Old Boy**'s gone deep into the unknown. Session ends with the team about to throw down.
