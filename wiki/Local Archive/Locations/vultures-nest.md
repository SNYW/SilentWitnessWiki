# The Vultures Nest

**Tags:** Vultures-Nest, Safehouse, Workshop, The-Time-of-the-Red, The-Precipice, Vultures-Turf, Crew-Allied

## Overview

The Vultures' base of operations in the Precipice. A converted cargo container community serving as living quarters, workshop, and medical facility.

## Description

- **Type:** Cargo container community / Base
- **Location:** The Precipice, Old Japantown
- **Threat Rating:** High

### Layout

- **Living Area**: Run-down living quarters where Magpie and Bekah sleep on the floor
- **Workshop**: Another container converted into Magpie's tech lab
- **Connection**: A hole cut between the two containers for access

## Residents

- **Magpie** — Techie / Cyberware specialist
- **Bekah "Bluejay"** — Combat member

## Facilities

- Living quarters (simple, floor sleeping)
- Workshop / Lab (Magpie's tech operations)
- Makeshift ripper clinic

## Session 3

Raptor was brought here after being injured by Mr Giggles. Emergency stabilization was performed to save his life.

## Notes

- Home base for the Vultures
- Safe haven for the party
