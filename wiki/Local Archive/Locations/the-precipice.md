# The Precipice

**Tags:** Location, Combat-Zone, Old-Japantown, Safehouse, Threat-High, Radiation-Hazard

## Basic Info
- **Type:** Cargo container community
- **District:** Old Japantown
- **City:** Night City
- **Status:** Active

## Overview

The Precipice is a cargo container community located in the Old Japantown district of central Night City. It's situated very close to the **Hot Zone**, which makes radiation exposure a constant concern for its residents.

## Key Features

- **Radiation Risk:** Due to its proximity to the Hot Zone, residents live in constant fear of radiation poisoning
- **Crisis Medical Center:** The nearby hospital offers radiation check-ups for residents
- **Human Experiments:** The Crisis Medical Center pays extra cash to residents who volunteer for experiments

## Campaign Relevance

- **Vultures Nest:** Magpie's cargo container home (CC05) is located here. This serves as the Vultures' base of operations.
- **Session 1:** The Bozos were harassing Magpie in this area before the confrontation at the transformer
- **Session 3:** Raptor was brought back here after being injured by the Bozos
