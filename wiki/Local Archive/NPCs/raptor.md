## Overview

Raptor is the leader of the Vultures gang. He leads from the front, often taking on dangerous missions personally.

## Session 3

During a meeting at **Dice or Die** casino, **Mr Giggles** (Bozos 2nd in command) interrupted and threw Raptor into the room. He was left missing an arm and an eye.

### Aftermath
- The party saved Raptor
- Carried him back to the **Vultures Nest** (Magpie's container)
- Emergency stabilization saved his life
- Currently recovering

## Status

Injured but stable. The party saved his life.
