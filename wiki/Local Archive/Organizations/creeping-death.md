# Creeping Death


## Basic Info
- **Name:** Creeping Death
- **Type:** Night Market Team / Poison Specialists
- **Status:** Active
- **Territory:** Unknown — operate from hidden labs

## Overview

Creeping Death deals in poisons — both delivery systems and the poisons themselves. They supply toxins to other Night Market teams and sometimes sell cures to those who can afford them. Deadly and professional.

## Known Members

### Leadership
- **Toxica** — Team Leader. Former biotechnica researcher. Speaks in riddles about "the sweet death."

### Subordinates
- 4 unknown members

## Known Activities

- Poison manufacturing
- Poison delivery systems
- Occasionally selling antidotes (at high prices)

## Notable Jobs

- **The Dinner Party:** Poisoned an entire corporate board meeting at a charity gala. Every executive collapsed. They sold "cures" to families for millions before revealing it was a bluff — none were lethal. Profited twice.

## Relationships

- **Neutral:** Other Night Market teams (supply side)
- **Hostile:** Law enforcement, Trauma Team
