# The ModLits


## Basic Info
- **Name:** The ModLits
- **Type:** Night Market Team / Remote Warfare Specialists
- **Status:** Active
- **Territory:** Industrial sectors, abandoned factories

## Overview

The ModLits (short for "Modified Little Things") specialize in remote warfare — mines, drones, and any weapon they can deploy and forget. They're the weapon dealers for the Night Market, providing tech to those who can't get it themselves.

## Known Members

### Leadership
- **Pins** — Team Leader. Cybernetic specialist with implant addiction. Never goes outside without a minimum of a dozen drones.

### Subordinates
- 3 unknown members

## Known Activities

- Drone manufacturing
- Mine deployment
- Remote weapon systems

## Notable Jobs

- **The Shopping District Incident:** Triggered a cascade of hidden mines during Black Friday shopping. No deaths — just chaos and panic — but cleaned out every storefront while everyone was distracted by "emergencies."

## Relationships

- **Neutral:** Other Night Market teams (supply side)
- **Hostile:** Anyone who owes them money
