# The Vultures


## Basic Info
- **Name:** The Vultures
- **Type:** Scavenger Gang
- **Status:** Active
- **Location:** The Precipice, Night City
- **Base of Operations:** Vultures Nest (Magpie's container home)

## Overview

The Vultures are a scrappy scavenger gang operating out of the Precipice district. They specialize in scavenging wasteland sites and scrap yards, picking clean anything of value from the ruins of Night City. Despite their rough exterior, they've developed a tight-knit family dynamic.

## Gang Structure

### Leadership
- **Vulture** — Gang Leader. The overall boss of the Vultures.
- **Raptor** — Right-hand man to Vulture. Leads operations in the field. Currently recovering from injuries (lost an arm and eye from Bozos attack).

### Core Members
- **Magpie** — Tech specialist and de facto second-in-command. Runs the Vultures Nest workshop/ripper lab.
- **Bekah "Bluejay"** — Magpie's sister. Combat-ready member, serves as scout and flanker.

### Support
- **Sparrow** — Young local boy who works as a messenger for the Vultures.

## Hired Security

The Vultures occasionally hire external help for dangerous jobs:
- **Roadblock (Ronnie Pickering)** — Veteran defensive specialist, former NCPD riot control. Works directly for Magpie.
- **Old Boy (Gallahan Murtock)** — Detective and gunslinger, hunting down those who wronged his family. Works for the Vultures.

## Territory

- **Vultures Nest** — Magpie's container home in the Precipice. Functions as living area, workshop, and ripper lab.
- The Precipice district

## Activities

- Scavenging wasteland sites
- Scrapping abandoned buildings and scrap yards
- Tech repairs and cyberware work
- Occasional mercenary work

## Relationships

### Allied
- **Roadblock** — Hired security
- **Old Boy** — Hired security
- Various local residents who benefit from Vulture charity

### Enemies
- **Bozos** — Rival gang, attacked Raptor and cost him an arm and eye
- **Scavenger groups** — Competition for scrap

## Notable Events

- **Treasure Map Incident** — Vultures acquired a mysterious map leading to a transformer site in the wasteland
- **Bozos Attack** — Raptor was ambushed and nearly killed by Bozos second-in-command Mr Giggles
- **Cryo-Pod Discovery** — Party discovered a mysterious girl in a cryo-pod during a Vulture job
