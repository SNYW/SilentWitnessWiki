# Toecutters

**Tags:** Time-of-the-Red, Night-City, Gang, Organization, Nomad


**Type:** Nomad Gang (Raffen Shiv)  
**Territory:** Badlands, Eastern edges of Night City, North Heywood  
**Threat Level:** High

## Overview

The Toecutters are a clan of Raffen Shiv operating in the Badlands and the eastern edges of Night City. They have established a notorious enough influence in North Heywood, making them a gang to be reckoned with in the RED era.

## Rumors and Reputation

The Toecutters have earned a fearsome reputation throughout the Night City area. They are rumored to be cannibals, adding to their terrifying mystique. This reputation serves them well in intimidation and territory control.

## Source

- Night City 2045 Atlas (R. Talsorian Games, 2025) - pp.12, 16

## Notes

- The Toecutters are likely named after and based on Toecutter, the main villain from the film Mad Max.
- They are part of the Raffen Shiv - rogue nomads who have been exiled from their original nomad nations.
