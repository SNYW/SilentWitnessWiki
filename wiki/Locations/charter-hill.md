# Charter Hill

**Tags:** District, Location, Charter-Hill


**District Code:** Q  
**Type:** Rebuilding Urban Zone  
**Threat Rating:** Low

## Overview

A small but influential community. On a clear day, the residents of Charter Hill can see the walls of the Exec Zone and dream of climbing even higher on the Corporate ladder.

## Government

- **City Manager:** Symon Featherstonehaugh (elected by property owners)
- **Security Provider:** Militech
- **Gangs Present:** Prime-Time Players, Tombstone Preservers, Tyger Claws

## Locations

### (Q1) Anjelika's
A host and hostess bar catering to clients with a heavy cyberware fetish.

### (Q2) Bella Mia
A club where the affluent and most fashionable residents of Night City go to be seen.

### (Q3) L'Ermitage
A high-end apartment complex. Most of the units are rented on a short-term basis by Execs visiting Night City for a prolonged period of time.

### (Q4) Seral Grove
An art installation turned high-end burial ground located beneath the district. Burial packages include digital reconstructions of the deceased so visitors can "speak" to their loved ones.
