# The Muses



**Type:** Posergang  
**District:** North Heywood (Woodland Park)  
**Threat Rating:** Low-Medium  

## Overview

The Muses are a posergang that mix 1980s big-hair styles and legwarmers with ancient Grecian fashion sensibilities. Based in the Woodland Park neighborhood of North Heywood, they are known for their roller derby team and entertainment sector presence.

## Appearance

Members are bodysculpted for the wide-eyed, high cheekbone look, and favor high perms and mullets. They like to wear tight clothing and are expert skaters, often carrying oversized, shoulder-mounted radios blasting cheerful pop electronic music.

## Headquarters

Their base of operations is **Xanadu**, a converted shipping depot that serves as their headquarters, roller rink, and nightclub. It was built to participate in the roller derby Night City Wonderland League under the name of Woodland Park Muses.

## Organization

- **Leader:** Calliope (captain)
- **Colors:** White and gold
- **Logo:** Stylized lyre

## Activities

The Muses host parties in their club/rink and are a common sight in Woodland Park. They are well known for their rhinestone-studded razzle-dazzle on the roller track. This income helps them pay their bills, roller equipment, and salaries.

Information from Cyberpunk RED sourcebooks confirming this as a Time of the Red (2045) organization.
