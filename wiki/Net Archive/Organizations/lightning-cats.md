# Lightning Cats

**Type:** Gang
**Territory:** Hot Zone (The Island)
**Threat Level:** Extreme

## Overview

Lightning Cats are a gang operating within the Hot Zone during the Time of the Red. They are one of the most notorious groups that have claimed territory in this devastated area of Night City.

## Description

The Lightning Cats have established themselves among the ruins of the Hot Zone, making their home in the wreckage of what was once Night City's gleaming corporate district. Like other Hot Zone inhabitants, they survive through salvage operations, scavenging valuable tech from the debris, and various illegal activities.

The gang takes their name from their distinctive visual aesthetic — members often incorporate lightning bolt designs into their cyberware and clothing, possibly reflecting a preference for electrical weapons and tech.

## Territory

- Hot Zone (throughout)
- Old Bank Block area
- Various ruined buildings

## Activities

- Salvage operations in radioactive areas
- Tech smuggling
- Protection rackets for other scavver groups
