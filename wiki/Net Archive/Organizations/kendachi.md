# Kendachi

**Tags:** Corporate

**Type:** Corporation (Arms Manufacturer)
**Headquarters:** Tokyo, Japan
**Founded:** 21st Century
**Era:** RED Era (2045)

## Overview

Kendachi (Kenshiri-Adachi Armaments) is a Japanese arms manufacturer specializing in advanced melee weapons and orbital manufacturing. While not a megacorporation or zaibatsu, Kendachi is part of the keiretsu system and operates globally with offices in major cities worldwide, including Night City.

By 2045, Kendachi was one of the few companies with orbital factories, being the world's top producer of orbital crystal and glass-fiber - a process that continued until 2045.

## Products

Kendachi is best known for its line of high-tech melee weapons:

### Monoweapons
- **MonoKatana** - Signature katana with monomolecular blade
- **MonoTwo** - Dual-blade variant
- **Monoknife** - Compact melee weapon
- **Monosword Cane** - Concealed weapon design
- **M-33 Powersword** - Heavy powered blade
- **M-94 Rapier** - Precision blade weapon
- **Monowire** - Flexible mono-filament weapon
- **Monowhip** - Experimental melee weapon

### Flame Weapons
- **F-253 Heavy Flamethrower**
- **Dragon Handheld Flamethrower**

## Night City Presence

Kendachi has maintained a regional office in Night City since the early 2000s. During the RED era, their products are popular among solos, martial artists, and anyone seeking high-quality melee weapons. The monoblade series has become synonymous with the corporation.

## Notable Facts

- Originates from 16th-century Japanese blacksmithing tradition
- Merged with Adachi company in the 21st century
- Operates orbital factories for crystal and glass-fiber production
- Known for monomolecular blade technology
- Products popular with solos and martial artists worldwide
