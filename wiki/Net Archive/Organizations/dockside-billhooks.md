# Dockside Billhooks

**Tags:** Dockside-Billhooks, Roller-Derby, Night-City-Wonderland-League, Heywood, Upper-Marina, Sports-Team

**Overview**

The Dockside Billhooks are a roller derby team in the Night City Wonderland League, active during the Time of the Red (2040s).

## Description

- **Type:** Sports Team / Gang-adjacent Organization
- **Home Territory:** Upper Marina and Heywood Industrial Zone
- **League:** Night City Wonderland League
- **Founded:** Early 2040s

## Background

The Dockside Billhooks are comprised primarily of dock workers from the Upper Marina and Heywood Industrial Zone areas. They train in an old container yard, utilizing the industrial infrastructure of the waterfront district.

## Characteristics

The Billhooks have earned a reputation for:
- **Homebrewed Intoxicants** — They sell homemade alcoholic beverages and drugs, a significant source of income
- **Gambling Operations** — Heavily involved in underground betting on their matches and other sporting events
- **Violence** — Known for violent behavior both on and off the roller derby track

## Territory

The team operates out of an old container yard in the industrial waterfront area, utilizing converted shipping containers for their headquarters, training facilities, and meetup locations.

## Notable Facts

- Part of the 12 official teams recognized by the Night City Wonderland League as of 2045
- Their violent reputation makes them a dangerous opponent in the league
- The combination of their dock work backgrounds and roller derby creates a rough, working-class image
