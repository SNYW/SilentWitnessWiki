# Tom's Diner



**Type:** Restaurant  
**Area:** Little China (Watson)  
**Threat Rating:** Low

## Overview

Tom's Diner is a retro-style restaurant located in Little China, in the Watson district of northern Night City. Known for its nostalgic pre-Collapse ambiance, the diner is famous for its 24/7 operations and giant neon burger sign atop the building.

## History

Founded and operated by the legendary Tom himself, the diner has been a staple of the Little China community for decades. The restaurant survived the turmoil of the Fourth Corporate War and the Time of the Red, continuing to serve its signature comfort food to edgerunners, fixers, and civilians alike.

## Atmosphere

Inside, the diner features a classic checkerboard floor, booth seating, and stools at the counter. The retro aesthetic harkens back to the old world before the Collapseâ€”a rare treat in Night City. The giant neon "Tom's Diner" sign is visible from several blocks away.

## Reputation

Tom's Diner is considered one of the few neutral ground locations in Night City where different factions can sit down together without immediate conflict. The diner is known for:

- Being open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week
- Rarely ever closing its doors
- Attracting a diverse clientele from edgerunners to NCPD officers
- Serving as a meeting point for various deals and exchanges

## Notable Staff

- **Tom** - Owner and cook, a Night City institution
- **Sergio** - Cook

## Notes

- The diner is occasionally visited by MaxTac operators and NCPD officers
- Despite its central location, the diner maintains a surprisingly safe reputation
- Many important deals have been made over Tom's coffee
