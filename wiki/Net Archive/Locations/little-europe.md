# Little Europe

**Tags:** District, Location, Little-Europe


**District Code:** A  
**Type:** Rebuilding Urban Zone  
**Threat Rating:** Medium

## Overview

A district divided between classes and times, with old brick buildings standing next to futuristic skyscrapers.

## Government

- **City Manager:** Amanda Polishchuk (elected by property owners)
- **Security Provider:** NCPD
- **Gangs Present:** 6th Street, Eurotrashers, Maelstrom, Undertow

## Locations

### (A7) Fiddler's Green
An "Irish" pub that's about as Irish as green beer on Saint Patty's day.

### (A8) Greta's
The best pool hall in Night City, with a strong lesbian client base.

### (A9) Holy Angels Church
A Catholic church run by Father Kevin and Father Paul. Known far and wide as neutral ground in Night City.

### (A10) Paradiso Terrestre
An upscale Southern Neo-Italian dining experience. Meals by appointment only.

### (A11) Short Circuit
Night City's premiere Tech and Netrunner bar. Owned and operated by Brain and his husband 3-Piece.

### (A12) Soprano's
An Italian restaurant that leans heavily into its mafioso theming. The real mob gives it a pass.

### (A13) Torrell and Chiang's
Considered by many to be the best tailors in Night City.
