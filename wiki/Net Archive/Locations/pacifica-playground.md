# Pacifica Playground

**District Code:** W  
**Type:** Combat Zone  
**Threat Rating:** High

## Overview

A district built around Playland by the Sea.

## Government

- **City Manager:** Elliot Kane (appointed by Playland by the Sea management)
- **Security Provider:** Militech
- **Gangs Present:** 6th Street, The Andersons, The Enhanced, Mudang Gumi, Piranhas, Voodoo Boys

## Locations

### (W1) Bits'n'Bolts
A hole-in-the-wall tech shop with connections to weaponsmith and inventor Faisal.

### (W2) Cubeland by the Sea
A shoddy cube hotel a stone's throw away from Playland by the Sea.

### (W3) Mister Rice Guy
An upscale sushi restaurant. Famous for its mascot, Hime Cat.

### (W4) Playhouse
An apartment complex with a shady past. Rumors suggest it is haunted.

### (W5) Playland by the Sea
Night City's premiere tourist destination. An amusement park sponsored by various Corporations. The Piranahs hold sway here, and it is home to a surprising number of yogangs (often used by the park as free labor).

### (W6) Pleasant Valley Apartments
An apartment building designed for neo-luddites who want to escape from technoshock overload.

### (W7) Scenic Cubes
A cube hotel covered in some of the most expressive graffiti in Night City.

### (W8) The XX (The Twenty)
A dive punk bar. Breakfist, the owner, serves juice instead of booze.
