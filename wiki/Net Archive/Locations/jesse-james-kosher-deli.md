# Jesse James' Kosher Deli

**Tags:** Jesse-James-Kosher-Deli, Restaurant, Survival-Spot, Combat-Zone, The-Time-of-the-Red, Threat-High


**Type:** Restaurant / Survival Spot  
**Area:** Old Combat Zone (District K)  
**Threat Rating:** High

## Overview

Jesse James' Kosher Deli is a unique eating establishment located in the Old Combat Zone district of central Night City. During the Time of the Red, this restaurant became famous for serving authentic Jewish cuisine in one of the most dangerous areas of the city.

## History

The deli was established by Jesse James (no relation to the infamous outlaw) in the early 2040s during the post-Holocaust reconstruction period. After the devastation of the Fourth Corporate War, Jesse saw an opportunity to serve the community in the Combat Zone with traditional comfort food that reminded survivors of better times.

## Atmosphere

The deli maintains a rough but welcoming atmosphere popular with:
- Local residents of the Combat Zone
- Edgerunners looking for a safe meal
- Survivors seeking a taste of pre-war culture

## Notable Features

- **Authentic Menu:** Known for serving traditional Jewish dishes including pastrami, corned beef, and matzo ball soup
- **Community Hub:** The deli serves as a gathering place for local residents
- **Combat Zone Location:** Situated in one of the most dangerous areas of Night City, making it a risky but memorable dining experience

## Connections

Jesse James' Kosher Deli is located in the Old Combat Zone, near other establishments like the Gen Red Clubhouse and The Underground. The area is controlled by various gangs, but the deli maintains a reputation as neutral ground.
