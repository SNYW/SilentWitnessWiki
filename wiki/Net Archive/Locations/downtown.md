# Downtown

**District Code:** C  
**Type:** Rebuilding Urban Zone  
**Threat Rating:** Low

## Overview

A new district carved out of Little Europe by Mister Kernighan and a consortium of Fixers and Execs known as the Chamber of Commerce. Downtown is preparing for Night City's full rebirth by creating the most commercially friendly district on the Island.

## Government

- **City Manager:** Emilia Ortega (appointed by Chamber of Commerce)
- **Security Provider:** Whitewater Security
- **Gangs Present:** Eastern Tigers Triad, Skiv Family, Undertow

## Locations

### (C1) Acadia Way
A single street containing restored bungalows, gated away from the rest of the world.

### (C2) Continental Brands Offices
The Night City headquarters of food juggernaut Continental Brands. Also home to the Oasis Megamart, the largest grocery store in the city.

### (C3) Cortex Complex
An ugly office building that vaguely resembles a human brain. Home to Jack Skorkowski Real Estate and DizCom, an industrial design firm.

### (C4) Delirium
A gothpunk virtuality club.

### (C5) Night City Firestation #2
The most well-funded firestation in Night City. It is well fortified, well equipped, and employs a number of FBC firefighters.

## Notes

Downtown represents Night City's aspirations for rebirth and commercialization. It's being deliberately developed as a business-friendly zone.
