# Viktor's Clinic

**Tags:** Ripperdoc, Little-China, Watson, Threat-Low, Shop

**Type:** Ripperdoc Clinic  
**District:** Little China, Watson  
**Threat Rating:** Low

## Overview

Viktor Vektor's clinic is one of the most well-known ripperdoc establishments in Night City's RED era. Located in Little China, the clinic serves edgerunners, corporate operatives, and civilians seeking quality cyberware installation and maintenance at reasonable prices.

## History

### The RED Era Beginning

Viktor Vektor established his clinic in Little China during the early 2040s, when the district was still known for its high-end medical facilities. As the neighborhood transitioned to a more working-class area, Viktor adapted his practice to serve the changing clientele.

### Reputation

By 2045, Viktor's Clinic has earned a reputation for:
- Affordable but quality cyberware installations
- Discrete services for clients who prefer anonymity
- A policy of treating anyone who can pay, regardless of gang affiliation
- Refusing to work on cyberpsychos or those showing signs of mental instability

## Services

- **Cyberware Installation:** Standard and experimental cyberware
- **Cyberware Removal:** Safe removal of incompatible or malfunctioning implants
- **Maintenance and Repair:** Regular servicing of existing cyberware
- **Medical Treatment:** Basic medical care alongside cybernetics
- **Neural Link Services:** Installation and configuration of neural interfaces

## Notable Features

- Located on the second floor above a noodle shop
- Well-worn but clean interior with examination chairs
- Comprehensive tool selection for various procedures
- Viktor maintains connections to black market suppliers for rare cyberware
- Payment plans available for loyal customers

## The Owner

**Viktor Vektor** is a veteran ripperdoc with decades of experience. He:
- Prefers cash payment but accepts some IOUs from trusted clients
- Maintains strict ethical boundaries (no work on children without guardian consent, no military-grade weapons tech)
- Has seen many clients come and go—some to fame, most to grave
- Often offers sage advice along with his medical services

## Notes

- Viktor's Clinic represents the backbone of Night City's medical infrastructure during the RED era—small, personal, and reliable
- The clinic is a common meeting spot for edgerunners seeking work
- Viktor's prices are competitive, making his services accessible to up-and-coming mercs
- He maintains a quiet professional demeanor and rarely asks questions about where clients got their injuries
