# Deravaja Dojo

**Tags:** Shop, Little-China, Watson, Threat-Medium

**Type:** Martial Arts Dojo  
**District:** Little China, Watson  
**Threat Rating:** Medium

## Overview

Deravaja Dojo is a martial arts training facility located in Little China. During the RED era, it serves as a training ground for edgerunners, bodyguards, and martial arts enthusiasts seeking to improve their combat skills.

## History

### Foundation

The dojo was established in the late 2030s by a group of former corporate security personnel who wanted to preserve traditional martial arts while adapting them for modern combat. The name "Deravaja" comes from a fictional ancient martial art created by the founders.

### Evolution

By the 2040s, the dojo had evolved to offer practical combat training for Night City's mercenary community. It became known for teaching techniques that could be used with or without cyberware enhancement.

## Training Programs

- **Basic Combat Arts:** Foundational techniques for beginners
- **Combat:** Complex moves for experienced fighters
- **Cyberware Integration:** Training that incorporates implanted enhancements
- **Weapons Training:** Melee weapons, from knives to monowire
- **Bodyguard Training:** Protection techniques for VIP security

## Notable Features

- Main training hall with padded floors
- Weaponry collection for training purposes
- Shower and changing facilities
- Small meditation room
- Observation gallery for clients watching training sessions

## Instructors

The dojo employs several instructors, each specializing in different combat styles. The head instructor, known simply as **Master**, rarely speaks but is rumored to have military special forces background.

## Reputation

Deravaja Dojo is known for:
- Real-world applicable combat training
- Accepting clients from all backgrounds, including gang members
- Reasonable pricing for quality instruction
- A code of conduct that forbids using training for senseless violence

## Notes

- The dojo maintains neutrality in gang disputes
- Some Tyger Claws members train here, creating interesting tensions
- It's a common meeting place for mercs seeking to improve their skills
- The dojo has survived multiple gang wars in the area due to its neutral stance
