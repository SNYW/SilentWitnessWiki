# Megabuilding H10

**District:** Watson Development  
**Type:** Residential Megabuilding  
**Threat Rating:** Low to Medium

## Overview

Megabuilding H10 is one of the first modern residential megabuildings constructed in Night City during the early 2040s as part of the Rebuilding Urban Center initiative. Located in the Watson Development district, H10 was at least partially operational by 2045, making it one of the earliest completed megabuildings in the city.

## History

The first modern residential megabuildings of Night City started construction in the Watson Development during the early 2040s. H10 was among the earliest completed structures, representing a new generation of multipurpose arcologies designed to function as cities within a city.

By 2045, H10 was partially open and quickly gained a reputation as a hub for nightlife and entertainment. The rooftop became notorious for DJ parties held every Saturday by 98.6 Shiv FM, a local radio station that broadcasted pop-techno from the top of the megablock.

## Features

### Residential
- Approximately 2,000 apartments with standardized layouts
- Upper floors feature luxury penthouses
- Middle floors offer spacious apartments with private bathrooms and kitchens
- Lower floors provide more affordable, cramped housing options
- All apartments include wall-mounted vending machines and direct cable access

### Services
- Food courts and market stalls
- Gym facilities
- Shooting range
- Various retail stores
- Centralized entertainment hubs

### Structure
- Multiple elevator systems (lobby access to residential floors, parking access)
- Underground parking garage spanning three levels
- Rooftop access for events and broadcasting

## Notable Features

The rooftop DJ parties hosted by 98.6 Shiv FM were a defining feature of H10 in the RED era, drawing crowds from across Watson Development every Saturday night. These parties helped establish the megabuilding's reputation as a center of youth culture and entertainment.

## Gangs & Organizations

- **98.6 Shiv FM:** Radio station broadcasting from the rooftop, hosting weekly DJ parties
- **Tyger Claws:** Presence in the surrounding Watson Development area

## Notes

Megabuilding H10 represents the beginning of Night City's transformation into a city of megabuildings. The success of H10 and similar structures paved the way for future megabuildings that would eventually dominate the city skyline.
