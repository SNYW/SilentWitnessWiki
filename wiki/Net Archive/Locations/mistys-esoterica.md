# Misty's Esoterica

**Tags:** Shop, Little-China, Watson, Threat-Low

**Type:** Tarot and Divination Shop  
**District:** Little China, Watson  
**Threat Rating:** Low

## Overview

Misty's Esoterica is a small tarot card and divination shop tucked away on a side street in Little China. During the RED era, it serves as a refuge for edgerunners, fixers, and civilians seeking guidance in uncertain times.

## History

### Origins

The shop was established by Misty in the early 2040s as Night City recovered from the devastation of the Fourth Corporate War. She built her clientele through accurate predictions and word-of-mouth recommendations among the mercenary community.

### Reputation

By 2045, Misty's Esoterica has become known for:
- Accurate tarot readings that seem to predict real-world outcomes
- A neutral ground where rival edgerunners can meet safely
- Honest readings—even when the news is bad
- Discreet services for those who prefer not to be seen at fixers' offices

## Services

- **Tarot Readings:** Standard and detailed readings for clients seeking guidance
- **Palm Reading:** Traditional fortune-telling for those who prefer it
- **Fortune Cookies:** Pre-packed fortunes sold to local restaurants
- **Talismans and Charms:** Small items believed to bring luck
- **Meditation Guidance:** Simple techniques for stress relief

## Notable Features

- Small shop with velvet curtains and incense smoke
- Collection of tarot decks, runes, and divination tools
- Back room available for private readings
- Bulletin board with job postings (handled discreetly)
- Tea and water available for clients

## The Owner

**Misty** is the enigmatic owner and tarot reader. She:
- Rarely reveals personal information about herself
- Seems to know more about clients than she should
- Maintains strict neutrality in gang disputes
- Has predicted several major events in Night City's underworld
- Appears to have connections to unknown powerful entities

## Clientele

The shop attracts a diverse clientele:
- Edgerunners seeking guidance before dangerous jobs
- Fixers looking for omens before major deals
- Civilians hoping for good news in troubled times
- Those curious about the supernatural

## Notes

- Misty's Esoterica represents the spiritual needs of Night City's population during the RED era
- Many clients report that her predictions come true with unsettling accuracy
- The shop operates on a "pay what you can afford" basis for readings
- Misty never predicts death—only "change"
