# Ling Po Public Library

**Tags:** Ling-Po-Public-Library, Library, Education, Little-China, Night-City

**Type:** Public Library  
**Location:** Floor 10, Guangbo Tower, Little China, Night City  
**Era:** Time of the Red (2045)

## Overview

The Ling Po Public Library is a community resource located on the tenth floor of Guangbo Tower in Little China. It was established as part of the district's revitalization efforts during the Time of the Red.

## History

The library was financed by David Ling Po, a city manager who invested in the future of Little China. The Little China Redevelopment Association named the establishment after him to honor his contribution to the district's rehabilitation.

By 2045, the library offered many services to the community, though it was only accessible to district residents.

## Services

The library provides:
- Public access terminals
- Physical book collection
- Community meeting spaces
- Educational programs for residents

## RED Era Significance

The Ling Po Public Library represents the effort to rebuild community resources in Little China during the post-Fourth Corporate War era. It serves as a symbol of the district's commitment to education and community development, even amid the challenges of the Time of the Red.
