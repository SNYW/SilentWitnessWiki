# Herschel's Crematorium

**Tags:** Herschels-Crematorium, Reclamation-Zone, Funeral-Home, The-Time-of-the-Red, Threat-Low


**Type:** Funeral Home / Cremation Services  
**Area:** Reclamation Zone  
**Threat Rating:** Low

## Overview

Herschel's Crematorium is a funeral and cremation services facility located in the Reclamation Zone of Night City. During the RED era, this establishment provides end-of-life services to families across the city, handling the deceased through cremation in an era when traditional burial is often impractical.

## History

The Reclamation Zone is one of the areas being slowly rebuilt after the devastation of the Fourth Corporate War. Herschel's Crematorium was established in the early years of the Time of the Red to address the practical needs of a city struggling with mass casualties and limited burial space.

The facility was founded by the Herschel family, who had backgrounds in both mortuary science and engineering. They designed the crematorium to be efficient and affordable, using salvaged equipment and modern cremation technology.

## RED Era Operations

During the 2040s, Herschel's provides:

- **Cremation Services:** Efficient cremation for individuals and mass casualty situations
- **Funeral Services:** Basic memorial services for families
- **Ash Processing:** Processing and packaging of cremains for family pickup
- **Death Documentation:** Official paperwork and death certificates for the city records

## Notable Features

- **Mass Capability:** Designed to handle large numbers of deceased during plague outbreaks or conflicts
- **Affordable Pricing:** One of the few affordable options for end-of-life services in Night City
- **Salvage-Based Design:** Built using repurposed industrial equipment

## Atmosphere

The facility has a somber, practical atmosphere:

- Clean but utilitarian waiting areas
- Simple memorial room for families
- Industrial cremation equipment
- Small staff of funeral directors and assistants

## Current Status

Herschel's Crematorium continues to operate throughout the RED era, serving families across Night City. The facility is busy during plague outbreaks and gang conflicts, when death rates spike.

## Reputation

The crematorium has a reputation for efficiency and discretion. While not glamorous, it provides a valuable service in a city where death is common and traditional burial is often impractical.

## Connections

- Located in the Reclamation Zone near other service businesses
- Works with hospitals and morgues across Night City for body pickup
- Competes with other funeral homes for business
- Connected to various religious and secular organizations for memorial services
