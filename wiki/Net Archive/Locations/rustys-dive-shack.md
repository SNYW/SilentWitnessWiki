# Rusty's Dive Shack

**Tags:** Time-of-the-Red, Port-of-Night-City, Location, Bar, Restaurant

**Type:** Bar / Dive Restaurant  
**Area:** Port of Night City  
**Threat Rating:** Medium

## Overview

Rusty's Dive Shack is a rough-and-tumble bar and food stall located in the Port of Night City area. During the RED era, this establishment serves dock workers, nomads, and seafarers who pass through the port, offering cheap drinks and simple food in a no-frills environment.

## History

The Port of Night City has long been a rough neighborhood, home to dock workers, smugglers, and travelers. Rusty's Dive Shack has been a staple of this area for decades, surviving the Fourth Corporate War and the economic upheaval that followed.

The bar takes its name from its original owner, a grizzled former dock worker known as "Rusty" who started the establishment as a simple food stall. Though Rusty himself has long passed, the name has stuck.

## RED Era Operations

During the 2040s, Rusty's Dive Shack operates as:

- **Bar:** Serving cheap drinks to dock workers and travelers
- **Food Stall:** Offering simple, hearty food at affordable prices
- **Information Hub:** A place where news and rumors about shipping, nomad movements, and job opportunities can be exchanged
- **Informal Meeting Spot:** Used by various groups for discrete business discussions

## Atmosphere

The bar has a worn, practical aesthetic:

- Basic wooden furniture, well-used and sometimes repaired
- Neon signs advertising drink specials
- Sticky floors and the persistent smell of stale beer and fried food
- A jukebox playing old-world music from before the Collapse
- A small stage for occasional live music performances

## Notable Features

- **Cheap Prices:** One of the few affordable places in the port area
- **Diverse Clientele:** Mixes dock workers, nomads, smugglers, and occasional tourists
- **Information Network:** Known as a place to hear rumors about shipping routes and job opportunities

## Current Status

Rusty's remains popular with its core struggles clientele but with the economic instability of the port area. The bar changes hands frequently and has seen several owners over the years, each adding their own touches while maintaining the basic dive shack aesthetic.

## Connections

- Located in the Port of Night City near Dock 13 and other port facilities
- Frequently visited by nomad packs passing through the area
- Known to have connections to smuggler operations
- Competes with The Randy Dandy and Flotsam for waterfront bar traffic
