# Highcourt Plaza Hotel

**Tags:** Time-of-the-Red, Old-Japantown, Location, Hotel,住宿

**Type:** Hotel / Accommodation  
**Area:** Old Japantown  
**Threat Rating:** Medium

## Overview

Highcourt Plaza Hotel is a hotel located in Old Japantown, one of the historic neighborhoods of Night City. During the RED era, this establishment offers basic but affordable accommodation to travelers, business visitors, and those seeking temporary housing in the rebuilding city.

## History

Old Japantown was originally settled by Japanese immigrants in the early 20th century and became a vibrant cultural hub. The neighborhood was devastated during the Fourth Corporate War but has been slowly rebuilding during the Time of the Red.

The Highcourt Plaza Hotel was originally built in the 1970s as a mid-range business hotel. It survived the war years in relatively good condition and has been operating continuously since, though with reduced services and amenities.

## RED Era Operations

During the 2040s, the Highcourt Plaza Hotel offers:

- **Standard Rooms:** Basic accommodation for travelers and business visitors
- **Extended Stays:** Weekly and monthly rates for those needing temporary housing
- **Meeting Rooms:** Small meeting spaces for business discussions
- **Restaurant:** Basic dining options for guests

## Atmosphere

The hotel maintains a faded elegance from its earlier days:

- Retro 1970s decor, showing signs of wear but still functional
- Smaller rooms than modern standards, typical of the era
- On-site restaurant serving Japanese and American cuisine
- Small business center with limited net access
- Parking available for guests with vehicles

## Notable Features

- **Affordable Rates:** One of the more reasonably priced hotels in the area
- **Old Japantown Location:** Walking distance to neighborhood restaurants and shops
- **Reliable Service:** Basic amenities consistently provided
- **Security:** Moderate security presence, safer than some alternatives

## Current Status

The Highcourt Plaza Hotel continues to operate throughout the RED era, catering to budget-conscious travelers and those doing business in Old Japantown. The hotel has maintained its basic services despite the economic challenges of the era.

## Reputation

The hotel has a reputation as a reliable, no-frills option in Old Japantown. It's not glamorous, but it provides clean, safe accommodation at reasonable prices—a valuable commodity in post-war Night City.

## Connections

- Located in Old Japantown near other businesses and restaurants
- Close to Crisis Medical Center for travelers needing medical attention
- Part of a network of independent hotels across Night City
- Accessible via NCART transit lines
