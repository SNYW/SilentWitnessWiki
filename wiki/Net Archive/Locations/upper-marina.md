# Upper Marina

**District Code:** B  
**Type:** Rebuilding Urban Zone  
**Threat Rating:** Medium

## Overview

A bustling district with a mix of old industrial zones and gentrified neighborhoods built around a series of docks and small marinas.

## Government

- **City Manager:** Kabria Chung (elected by property owners)
- **Security Provider:** NCPD
- **Gangs Present:** Maelstrom, Prime-Time Players, Street Queens

## Locations

### (A1) Camden Court
A secure apartment complex favored by Solos and Execs.

### (A2) Chopper's
Formerly a butcher's shop, now a bar. The meat grinder still works if you want to get rid of a body.

### (A3) Continental Brands Vertical Neighborhood
Housing for Continental Brands employees. A nightmare to navigate due to its "flavors of the world" theming.

### (A4) Cube-A-Rama
A cube hotel with an original brick exterior.

### (A5) Danger Gal Housing Facility
Housing for Danger Gal employees. Pink and full of life.

### (A6) Danger Gal Offices
Home base for Danger Gal, a private investigation and security Neocorp.

### (B1) The Afterlife
The premiere edgerunner bar in Night City. Owned by the legendary Solo, Rogue.

### (B2) Buffalo's
The latest in a long line of failing restaurants opened in this location. Serves global fusion cuisine.

### (B3) City Medical Center
An enormous hospital complex housing some of the best medical staff and technology in Night City.

### (B4) The Garden of Earthly Delights
A full hedonistic experience brothel. A visit starts with dinner and ends with time spent with meat puppets programmed to the client's specifications.

### (B5) GraffitiX
An art gallery displaying the work of Night City's best up-and-coming artists.

### (B6) La Lune Blue
A high-end French restaurant.

### (B7) Marina FloatHomes
A collection of small houseboats for rent.

### (B8) McCartney Cubes
A cube hotel near the stadium.

### (B9) McCartney Field Stadium
A 75,000 seat stadium and the heart of Night City's sports and concert scene.

**Franchises:**
- Slammers (Baseball)
- Heat (Basketball)
- Death Dealers (Combat Soccer)
- Nuke (Murderball)
- New Battlegrounds Championship Wrestling League

### (B10) Night City Bubbles
A high-end spa. Some employees can be hired as escorts for the evening.

### (B11) REO Meatwagon Offices
Home of Night City's second-biggest EMS operation.

### (B12) TravlStay CityCenter
A cube hotel with paper-thin walls and absolutely no security.

### (B13) Ziggurat Offices
The headquarters of Ziggurat, the company responsible for building and maintaining Citinets across the continent. Attached is the Hanging Gardens, which provides housing for employees.
