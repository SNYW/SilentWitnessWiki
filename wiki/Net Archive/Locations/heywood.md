# Heywood

**Tags:** Heywood, District, Corporate-Residential-Zone, Valentinos, 6th-Street, Threat-Medium

**District Code:** J  
**Type:** Corporate Residential Zone  
**Threat Rating:** Medium

## Overview

Heywood is one of Night City's oldest suburban districts, located in the southern portion of the metropolitan area. Originally developed in the late 20th century as a middle-class residential area, Heywood underwent significant changes during the Fourth Corporate War and the Time of the Red.

By the 2040s, Heywood had partially recovered but remained a contested territory. The district is known for its tough but working-class residents, established neighborhoods, and ongoing tension between corporate interests and local communities.

## Government

- **City Manager:** Appointed by Night City Council
- **Security Provider:** Valentinos (protection contracts), NCPD
- **Gangs Present:** Valentinos, 6th Street

## Notable Locations

### Corporate Center
The heart of Heywood's business district, featuring offices for various megacorps including Militech and other defense contractors.

### Heywood Entertainment District
Bars, clubs, and braindance parlors catering to Night City's nightlife scene.

### Residential Blocks
Various megabuildings and apartment complexes housing the district's diverse population.

## Economy

Heywood's economy includes:
- Corporate offices (Militech, others)
- Manufacturing and light industry
- Entertainment and nightlife
- Valentinos protection services
- 6th Street security contracts

## Notable Gangs

### Valentinos
The dominant gang in Heywood, operating as a neighborhood protection organization with deep roots in the Hispanic community. They provide security services and maintain order in their territory.

### 6th Street
A veteran gang with roots in the US military, operating in Heywood and providing security services. They have a complicated relationship with the Valentinos.

## Notes

Heywood represents the struggle of many Night City districts to recover in the RED era. While not as devastated as the Combat Zones, Heywood still bears scars from the Fourth Corporate War. The presence of both Valentinos and 6th Street provides security but also creates ongoing territorial tensions.

The district is known for its strong sense of community and resistance to corporate encroachment, making it a unique part of Night City's social landscape.
