# Port of Night City

**District Code:** J  
**Type:** Rebuilding Urban Zone  
**Threat Rating:** Medium

## Overview

A central location for cargo entering and leaving Night City by sea. Run by nomads from the Thelas Nation.

## Government

- **City Manager:** Calypso (appointed by Thelas elders)
- **Security Provider:** Thelas Marines
- **Gangs Present:** Consortium, DeadWoods

## Locations

### (J1) Dock 13
The storefront (and occasional night market) of marketing genius Willy "Mister Amaaaze" Maze.

### (J2) Dock 14 Studio Apartments
An apartment building just one dock away from the always loud Dock 13.

### (J3) Dock Cargo Community
A cargo container community nestled between the docks.

### (J4) Flotsam
A small drift town just off the coast. It consists of several larger vessels run aground connected by smaller boats, rafts, and walkways. The Randy Dandy, a mobile bar, often docks here.

### (J5) Medical Technologies
A body bank specializing in growing replacement limbs. Known for buying and selling dodgy cyberware as well.

### (J6) Rusty's Dive Shack
A rough-and-tumble bar for sea-faring types. Built out of the bridge of an old container sub.

## Notes

The Thelas Nation nomads run the port. They trade their technical and motor skills for land. It's a unique district with a strong nomad presence.
