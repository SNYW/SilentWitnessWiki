# Jig-Jig Street


**Type:** Red Light District / Entertainment Zone  
**District:** Watson, Northside  
**Threat Rating:** Medium

## Overview

Jig-Jig Street is a notorious entertainment district in Watson, known for its brothels, strip clubs, and adult venues. During the RED era, it operates as a relatively safe (by Night City standards) red light district catering to those seeking adult entertainment.

## History

### Origins

The street earned its name from the adult entertainment venues that have operated there since the late 20th century. Despite the devastation of the Fourth Corporate War, the adult entertainment industry persisted in Night City.

### RED Era Operations

By 2045, Jig-Jig Street has rebuilt and now operates under the protection of the Tyger Claws gang. It represents one of the few areas in Watson where adult entertainment can operate relatively openly.

## Venues

The street features various establishments:
- **Brothels:** Various venues offering companionship services
- **Strip Clubs:** Adult entertainment venues with dancers
- **Braindance Parlors:** Adult-themed virtual reality experiences
- **Sex Shops:** Stores selling adult products and toys

## Services

- **Companionship:** Professional services for those seeking company
- **Entertainment:** Live adult performances
- **Adult Braindance:** Virtual experiences of various kinds
- **Merchandise:** Adult products and novelty items

## Notable Features

- Neon signs advertising various adult services
- Security personnel at establishment entrances
- Tyger Claws patrols maintaining order
- Relatively clean streets compared to other entertainment areas

## Gangs Present

- **Tyger Claws:** Primary controlling gang, providing security and taking a cut of profits
