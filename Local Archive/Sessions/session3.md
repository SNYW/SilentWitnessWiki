# Session 3: Awakening


## Overview

The mysterious girl from the vault awakens, revealing strange abilities. Meanwhile, the Bozos make their move.

## Events

### The Girl Wakes Up
- The girl from the cryo-pod awakens
- She exhibits strange powers:
  - Nothing can properly touch her
  - She doesn't get wet in the rain
  - She leaves no footprints
- **Appearance**: White hair, grey skin
- Visibly interested in their technology
- **Magpie notices** text appearing in her eyes when scanning objects
- She types on her cryosleep casket, displaying the entire history of the area since around 2018 at superhuman speed

### The Vulture Summons
- Local boy known as **Sparrow** came to summon them for the Vultures
- They paid Sparrow **50eb** — very risky in the Precipice

### Confrontation at Dice or Die
- **Mr Giggles** interrupted the meeting at the casino (Dice or Die)
- Threw **Raptor** into the room — missing an arm and an eye
- Taunted the team: "They are now even"
- Challenge: If the party wants to take it up with them, come to their base — **the Big Top**

### Saving Raptor
- Raptor was saved by the party
- Carried him back to the **Vultures Nest** (Magpie's home container)
- Performed emergency stabilization
- Raptor survived

### The Scrap Site
- Party went to the scrap site
- Defeated local scavs who had already started scavenging the site
- Looted:
  - Several crap guns
  - Cyberware from dead scavs
  - A **prototype car** ruined in the area
  - Several key elements from the car
  - A **prototype AI self-driving module** — Magpie wants to use as a self-defense drone
